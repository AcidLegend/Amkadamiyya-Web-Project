Amkadamiyya School Jalingo

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Amkadamiyya School Jalingo which opened its doors in the year 2018 to families seeking for moral and sound Islamic and Western knowledge. Amkadamiyya School is a vibrant, dynamic, and progressive co-educational school located in the prime serene area of Samunaka Junction, Along Wuro Sembe Road.
Welcome on board!

Alh. Abubakar Muhammad Kabri - Director.

About Amkadamiyya

Amkadamiyya is a Science and Art school that sees a world where education is the key to virtue and success. Delivering knowledge of Islamic morals and ethics to develop the intellectual, ethical, and interpersonal foundations students need to be constructive contributors to the world.
